About

One day on June 7, 2026, a man named Jack Rivera woke up in the middle of the night. He doesn't know much, only this: that he wakes up in the night and has 10 years to live, with his timer starting whenever the night starts. He has less time, so he will enjoy whatever he can as much as he can, in as little time as he can.
